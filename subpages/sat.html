Ongoing SAT Study Plan

Daily Tasks (1-2 hours)

  • Review Mistakes: Spend 15-20 minutes analyzing previous questions you got wrong. College board site
  • YouTube Videos: Watch 1-2 educational videos daily on specific SAT topics. Find videos here.
  • Practice Questions: Complete 5-10 practice questions daily. Use official SAT materials.
  • Vocabulary Practice: Spend 10-15 minutes on vocabulary using flashcards or apps. Aim for 5 new words daily.Vocab Site
  • SAT Reddit: Browse the SAT subreddit for tips and community insights.

Weekly Tasks (3-4 hours)

  • Focused Study Sessions: Dedicate 1-2 hours weekly to specific topics using various resources.
  • Math and Reading/Writing Practice: Spend 30-60 minutes on these sections weekly, rotating focus.
  • Discussion/Group Study: Meet once a week to discuss challenging concepts.
  • Assessment: Take a short quiz at the end of the week to gauge your understanding.

Monthly Tasks

  • Full-Length Practice Test: Take one full-length practice test monthly.
  • Resource Review: Evaluate study materials and adjust as needed.
  • Content Review: Spend a few hours reviewing major concepts in each subject area.
  • Strategy Enhancement: Refine your test-taking strategies monthly.
  • Engagement with Reddit: Participate in discussions on the SAT subreddit.

Long-Term (Every 2-3 Months)

  • Content Mastery Check: Revisit each content area and ensure understanding.
  • Adjust Goals: Reflect on progress and set new goals for study sessions.
  • Mental Well-Being: Incorporate stress management techniques into your routine.

Final Thoughts

  • Consistency is Key: Regular practice will help improve your score.
  • Stay Flexible: Adjust your plan based on what is most effective for you.
  • Engage with the Community: Utilize SAT Reddit for support and motivation.
